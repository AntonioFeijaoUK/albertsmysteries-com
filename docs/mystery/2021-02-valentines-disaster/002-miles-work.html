002 Miles' work...

Miles doesn't talk much about his work, only that he is a civil servant for the International Trade Department, which is based at 3 Whitehall.

However, Miles has told Veronica that he is sometimes placed in another office, if she needs to reach him quickly.

He says it is the sister building of 12 Molendini ripae.

Veronica has never heard of this street but hasn't given it much thought.

Where does Miles really work?
