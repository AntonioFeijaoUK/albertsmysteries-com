005 Locker number...

At security row number 5, a man with (methylene) blue hair instructs Miles and Veronica to put their luggage in trays.

Veronica comments on the man's blue hair, and they have a laugh about it.

The man examines Miles' tray and while readjusting it, he slips two pieces of paper and a key access card into the tray.

Miles picks up his things from the tray after and quickly pockets the pieces of paper and key card.

Veronica tells Miles he is acting weird, and Miles tells her he just needs the toilet.

He goes there for some peace to look at the first piece of paper.

It says that what he needs is in an airport staff locker and a row of numbers:

3 15 14 2
11 6 5 12
5 9 13 7
15 4 2 13

What's the number on the locker Miles is looking for?
