007 Whitch gate...

Miles opens the padlock and retrieves a small bag with microchips, that fits in his jacket pocket.

Miles is hurrying back to Veronica before 10 minutes has elapsed, but he gets a feeling he is being followed...

Two guys, the first is wearing a black cap, grey T-shirt, jeans and white trainers.

The second guy has brown hair, black T-shirt, grey trousers and black trainers.

He reaches an angry Veronica, who asks Miles which gate they need to go to.

The two suspicious guys hover nearby, so Miles whispers to Veronica "00001100".

Which gate do they need to reach?
