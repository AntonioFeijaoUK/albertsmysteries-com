014 The two cities...

Miles is shocked, but he wakes up Veronica and they go for breakfast.

Veronica thinks they should stay in Munich for the rest of their time to relax, but Miles is very insistent that he really wants to take her to Budapest.

She eventually demurs and heads upstairs to pack.

Miles goes to check on the car but nearby he sees the two guys from the airport!

He hurries to tell Veronica the car has broken down and they need to go to Munich Central station to get a train.

They need to change trains twice.

Which two major cities are they crossing to reach Budapest?
