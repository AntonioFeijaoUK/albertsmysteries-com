018 Handover place...

As Miles and Veronica tuck into their meals, another waiter comes over and suggests the same 'methylene blue cake special'.

The waiter informs him that the microchips Miles handed over are untraceable, and he has effectively aided terrorism!

They must be returned but the waiter only knows that the microchips will be handed over where 'communism went to die by Stalin's boots'.
