019 Bluetooth Viking...

Miles tells Veronica that he is feeling unwell, but that she should stay to finish her meal.

Veronica protests but Miles is already gone with the waiter.

They break into a car and start driving to Memento Park, the waiter calling for back up from the embassy and Hungarian Police.

As they drive, the waiter says that it must be the traitors work, how else would the criminals know?

He informs Miles that the traitor is known as Bluetooth, named after a Viking, in criminal circles.

Can you guess who the traitor is?
