You are invited to take part in a special zoom (belated) Valentine mystery on 28th of Feb from 1600!

Also, don't forget that sustenance is required to solve mysteries.

We have a special pizza recipe from our resident Italian, Dani :)

The dough needs to be prepared in advance (you can cheat and use ready-made, but you will be judged) and we can all have a zoom pizza party in between solving the riddles.

Pizza dough recipe:

  • 400g flour, type 0
  • 7g dried yeast
  • 10g sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 230-250ml lukewarm water

Mix all together to form a dough.

Leave to rise for 1 hour.

After 1 hour, fold the dough in 3 (see below) and leave to rise for another hour.

Do this every hour for 3 hours.

Roll dough into two pizza bases.

Topping ideas (you can use whatever you want, but these are Italian-approved):
  • Passata (or small cherry tomatoes cut in pieces)
  • Mozzarella (squeeze the water out and grate it generously over the pizza)
  • Oregano
  • Speck and brie
  • Capers and anchovies
  • Baked ham and olives
  • Gorgonzola and radicchio


Cook until the dough is done and the cheese is melted on top.
