It's Friday afternoon and the team is having a relaxing game of dungeons and dragons!

Duncan's phone has audibly been going off a few times, but everyone has politely ignored it.

The 5th time his phone rings, Duncan pauses the game and goes on mute to take his phone call while the rest of the team ponder strategy and sipping drinks.

Five minutes later, Duncan comes back and excitedly tells everyone about an old friend from his oilrig days, Miles, who has asked for Duncan's help to get a package out of the oilrig.

Miles is staying on the oilrig for another month, but he was quite adamant that the package leaving is rather time sensitive.

Duncan trusts Miles explicitly, so has promised to help. As it is a bit of an undertaking, Duncan asks the whole team to come and help him!

Are you ready to help Miles?
