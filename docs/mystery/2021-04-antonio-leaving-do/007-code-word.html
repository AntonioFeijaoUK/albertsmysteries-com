The keys for a catamaran that Alex, an avid captain, will sail to the oilrig.

A slip of paper with a codeword to say to Miles so he knows he is handing the drone to Duncan and his team.

The team locate the catamaran and, with Alex barking orders, set to sea to reach the oilrig to obtain the drone from Miles.
